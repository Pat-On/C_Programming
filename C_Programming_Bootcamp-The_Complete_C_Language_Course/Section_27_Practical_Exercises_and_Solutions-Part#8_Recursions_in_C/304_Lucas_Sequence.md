# Lucas Sequence

Gemini said
The Lucas sequence (or Lucas numbers) is the "cousin" of the famous Fibonacci sequence. It follows the exact same rule—add the last two numbers to get the next one—but it starts with different numbers.

While Fibonacci starts with 0 and 1, the Lucas sequence starts with 2 and 1.

The First 10 Lucas Numbers

2, 1, 3, 4, 7, 11, 18, 29, 47, 76
